Profile

I'm a third-year Electronics & Communication Engineering student at Sri Eshwar College of Engineering, currently at a 7.4 CGPA through semester 3. My interest sits at the intersection of hardware and software — I like taking a circuit from a breadboard to a working, connected device, then writing the code that makes it useful. That's pulled me toward embedded systems and IoT: microcontroller programming, sensor interfacing and real-time data pipelines, backed by a working knowledge of C, Python and Java for the software side.

Outside coursework, I practice problem-solving daily on LeetCode and SkillRack, and I'm building a small portfolio of independent projects — from a personal finance tracker to an AI-assisted component management system — as a way of learning by shipping rather than just studying theory.
